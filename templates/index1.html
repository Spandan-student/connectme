Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify my university email?

After registration, we'll send a verification link to your university email address. Simply click the link to verify your account.

Can I join multiple teams?

Yes! You can join up to 5 active teams at once, allowing you to collaborate on different projects simultaneously.

How does the matching algorithm work?

Our matching algorithm analyzes your skills, interests, academic goals, and project preferences to suggest peers who would make great collaborators.

Is StudentConnect free for students?

Yes! StudentConnect is completely free for all university students with a valid .edu email address.
